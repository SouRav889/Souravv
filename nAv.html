NAVAJYOTHI COLLEGE

Navajyothi College of Arts and Science is an emerging centre for higher education situated at Kannikkalam near Cherupuzha in Kannur district and is managed by CST fathers. Navajyothi College of Arts and Science is also the best college in Cherupuzha in terms of infrastructure, faculty and academic pursuit.
